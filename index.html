Welcome to my website. My name is Kelly and I am a multidisciplinary digital designer. I love to form, create and make the world of screens a more fun and interesting place to live. Currently, I am pursuing a Master's degree at the University in Florida in Web Design and Online Communications. I have training in front-end and back-end design, although at the moment I favor coding. I find the mathematical approach of manipulating information to make it visually appealing both challenging and rewarding. Please take a look aroung my site. If you like what you see, please contact me. I am readily available to accept commissions.

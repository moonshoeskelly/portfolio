This is Me

I grew up in a San Francisco suburb called Burlingame. I played on my family's computer quite a bit growing up (despite the mind-numbing dial up noise every time I wanted to log on to AOL...). My college years took me to the east coast. First to Boston, and then to Florida. After graduation I began working seasonally in Alaska as a tour guide. I move around quite a bit, but I always have my computer. It is now a much better upgrade than what my family had growing up, although I am still not convinced that this touchbar is living up to it's hype. Nowadays, I play when I can, work when I must, and watch as much Netflix as my eyes allow. I'm basically just living a life of limbo while I wait for the final seasons of Game of Thrones to return.
